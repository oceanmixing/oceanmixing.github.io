For Wissenschaftsjahr

For a German-British celebration of Marine Science, Eleanor Frajka-Williams was profiled on her work in the Atlantic.

For Germany’s Year of Science 2016-17, Wissenschaftsjahr, the UK was the chosen partner for “Seas and Oceans”. Eleanor was profiled for their website. You can read it in original German, or English.

In German: https://www.wissenschaftsjahr.de/2016-17/das-wissenschaftsjahr/partnerland-vereinigtes-koenigreich/die-pumpe-im-atlantik.html

In English: https://www.wissenschaftsjahr.de/2016-17/weiterfuehrende-informationen/englisch/science-year-201617-seas-and-oceans/the-circulation-pump-in-the-atlantic.html

About This Site

The Ocean Dynamics and Mixing group at the University of Southampton is comprised of faculty, postdoctoral fellows and students. The focus of our group is on ocean observations with the aim of understanding these measurements and comparing with theoretical understandings of the ocean. More ›
